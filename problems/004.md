# Problem 004 — Largest Palindrome Product

A palindromic number reads the same both ways. The largest palindrome made from the product of two 2-digit numbers is 9009 = 91 × 99.

Find the largest palindrome made from the product of two 3-digit numbers.

Your script must print a single integer to stdout when run with `python solution.py`.

**Scoring:** `correctness / (1 + total_tokens / 10000)`, where `correctness` is 1.0 if the printed integer matches the expected answer exactly and 0.0 otherwise. `total_tokens` is the sum of agent and judge tokens across the /goal loop.
